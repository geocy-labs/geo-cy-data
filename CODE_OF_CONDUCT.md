# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive environment for collaborators and users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews
- Focus critiques on ideas, evidence, and implementation details
- Assume good intent while remaining clear and honest about technical issues

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, open a private maintainer contact channel through the repository hosting platform.
